01

What this extension does

AI Chat Transfer is a browser extension that lets you save conversations from AI platforms — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, DeepSeek, and Grok — and resume or continue them on a different platform. All of this happens locally inside your browser with no cloud backend of any kind.

02

What data we collect

We collect nothing. The extension does not transmit any data to any server controlled by us or any third party.

The only data that exists is what you choose to save, and it is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local).

Data type Where it lives Leaves your device?
Saved chat messages Browser local storage Never
Chat titles & timestamps Browser local storage Never
Source platform & URL Browser local storage Never
Your identity / email Not collected Never
Browsing history Not collected Never
03

Why the extension reads AI chat pages

The extension injects a small content script into the supported AI platforms listed in the manifest. This script does exactly two things:

Reading: When you click "Save," the script reads the visible conversation text from the page's DOM (HTML elements) and stores it locally in your browser.

Writing: When you choose "Continue here," the script injects the previously saved conversation as a text file into the target platform's file upload so that AI can pick up the conversation context.

04

Permissions explained

Permission Why it is needed
storage Saves your chats locally in the browser. No data leaves the device.
Host access to AI platforms Required to read the conversation DOM and inject the floating widget button on supported sites.
notifications (background) Shows brief status messages (e.g. "Chat saved") as Chrome notifications. Optional and local only.
web_accessible_resources Allows the floating popup panel (an iframe loading popup.html) to be rendered inside the AI platform pages.

The extension does not request access to tabs outside the listed AI platforms, your browsing history, your clipboard beyond what is needed for file injection, or any external API.

05

Third-party sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, license, or otherwise disclose your data to any third party. There are no analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers integrated into this extension.

The extension communicates with no external server, API, or web service of any kind.

06

Deleting your data

You are in full control of your data at all times.

To delete individual chats, use the delete (🗑) button inside the extension popup. To wipe all stored data at once, go to Chrome Settings → Privacy and security → Site data → Extensions → AI Chat Transfer → Clear data, or uninstall the extension, which removes all locally stored data automatically.

07

This extension is not directed at or intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
